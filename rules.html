Drunk Bus Driver Game Rules

The game consists of two phases, Hand and Bus.

Phase One: Hand

At the start of the game, there are four face-down cards in front of each player. The task is terribly simple: moving clockwise, one guess awaits each player per turn.

1.: Will the first card be black or red?

2.: Will the second card be smaller or larger than the first card?

3.: Will the value of the third card fall between or outside of the values of the two previously flipped cards?

4.: What is the exact value of the fourth card?

LIVER DAMAGE DIFFICULTY:

The color of the fourth card has to be guessed!

With a correct guess, you can give a sip, while with an incorrect guess, you have to drink a sip of your drink.

LIVER DAMAGE DIFFICULTY:

The number of sips increases with the number of cards: 1-2-3-4. Eg: for flipping the first card you can give a sip if you get it right, but for the second card you can give two sips.

Twist: If a player gets a card of the same value as any of his previous cards, the whole squad has to take a sip.

LIVER DAMAGE DIFFICULTY:

Everyone drinks a shot.

Phase Two: Bus

The bus consists of a drinker and a dealer column. Going from the first row, we turn over the drinker and dealer cards. On the bus, the number of seats (rows) is both the number of sips to be allocated and the number of sips to drink. If the value of a card on the bus equals the value of one of the cards in the players' hand, the player either has to drink or give sips. The number of times a card is in that person's hand, you need to multiply the number of sips.

Eg: There are four cards in front of George: 2 - 7 - 7 - K. In the third place of the dealer column, a 7 turns up â€”-> Normally, George would give 3 sips, but he has two sevens, so he can give 3x2 = 6 sips as he pleases.

It's important to know that sips can be further split, you don't have to give one person 6 sips, you can have three people drink 2 sips and so on.

As soon as all the drinker and dealer cards are turned over, all that is left is the bus driver.

LIVER DAMAGE DIFFICULTY:

There are two bus drivers.

Players with a card equal to the value of the bus driver are entitled to optionally compulsively down all the drink in their glass. In the event that the volume of the beverage in the players' glass is not sufficient for a fair drink-downing experience, the squad may vote for a refill.

WARNING!

RESPONSIBLE ALCOHOL CONSUMPTION IS VERY IMPORTANT!

DO NOT play this game or drink alcohol under the age of 18!

DO NOT drive under the influence of alcohol.

NO PLAYER should be forced to drink! If a player does not want to participate in the game, it is his decision that other players must respect.
